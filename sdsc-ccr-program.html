SDSC HPC Students
The UCSD Co-Curricular Record (CCR) (https://elt.ucsd.edu/ccr/index.html) highlights student achievements in opportunities beyond the classroom including a brief description and the skills developed on an official record. The purpose of the CCR is to demonstrate the value of engaging in opportunities beyond the classroom, and to help students reflect on and articulate the competencies and skills they developed.

Co-Curricular Record (CCR) - elt.ucsd.edu elt.ucsd.edu

The HPC Students Program has been working with the UCSD Co-Curricular Record team to create a system where SDSC staff and researchers can grant Co-Curricular Record credit to thier students. SDSC currently has one approved CCR, and 3 pending.
