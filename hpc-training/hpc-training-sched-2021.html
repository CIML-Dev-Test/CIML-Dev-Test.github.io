Training Session Details:

Participants will be given accounts on Expanse, the newest SDSC Supercomputer, and will be given the opportunity to complete several basic assignments. There is no grade in the course:

  • Participants who successfully complete the HPC Training program will receive an SDSC Certificate of Completion in HPC Training.
  • Students who complete the course will also be eligible to receive [UCSD Co-Curricular Credit]() for their efforts (see the SDSC CCR Program).

To sucessfully complete the course, participants must attend most of the sessions and complete approximately 75% of the work. Participants are encouraged to work as teams, and can work on the assignments past the last date of training sessions with the approval of the instructor.
